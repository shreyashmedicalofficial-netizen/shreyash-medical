Who We Are

Your Trusted Healthcare Partner Since 2010

Shreyash Pharmacy is more than just a pharmacy; we are a part of your family's health journey. Founded with a vision to make genuine medicines accessible to everyone in Kolhapur, we have grown into a trusted name for quality healthcare products and services.

Our team of experienced pharmacists is dedicated to providing you with the best advice and care. We believe in transparency, quality, and affordability. Whether you need prescription medicines, wellness products, or just some friendly health advice, we are here for you 24/7.
